Cereal

Description

Instructions on how to make a bowl of cereal

Ingredients

  • Any kind of cereal you like
  • Milk
  • Sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Pour the desired amount of cereal into a bowl.
  2. Pour the desired amount of milk onto the cereal, and absolutely do not do this step first.
  3. Add sugar if you want to, or don't. I don't know how you like your cereal.
